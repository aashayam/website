OUR AIMS AND OBJECTIVES
We believe our child strengths and abilities.

Aim: This is a non-profit service-oriented organization to serve the mentally retarded children poor, needy downtrodden people of the present society anywhere in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, and other parts of the country

Objectives:
  • To provide food, clothing, shelter and education to children who are mentally retarded children
  • To open schools and residential hostels for mentally challenged children
  • TO conduct personality development programmes
  • TO hold meeting/seminars/discussions from time to time to achieve higher level of educational values
  • To provide sheltered employment for mentally challenged children
  • To upgrade skills to enable them to start income generating activities on their own
  • To Assist the disability, mentally retarded, handicap people and to arrange for their education and skill development programme
  • Residential institution for children such as institutions for the care protection and rehabilitation of handicapped children
  • To provide guidance service for children including child guidance clinics family life institution
  • To establish institutions providing welfare service to the physically and mentally handicapped such as care maintenance, rehabilitation and recreational clubs for the care of physically and mentally handicapped person and institution for welfare activities such as assistance and guidance for the handicapped
  • TO run the educational society for fulfils. The educational of present and future society
  • To conduct vocational programmes
  • To promote national integration
